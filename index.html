About Me

I believe that the biggest challenges, whether in healthcare or technology, are solved by building better systems. My expertise lies in engineering scalable solutions for complex business challenges. I specialise in translating strategic vision into a ground-level execution, and unifying cross-functional teams to bring that vision to life.

I do my best work in a culture that values learning as much as outcomes, where teams are connected, autonomous, and use rapid experimentation to navigate uncertainty and deliver measurable results.

Blood Safety Initiative: Sierra Leone

Partners: Partners In Health (PIH), MSD for Mothers

The Challenge

To improve maternal health outcomes in Sierra Leone by providing reliable, local access to safe blood in a region with limited advanced health infrastructure.

My Role & Approach

I led this project from the ground up, turning an idea into a fully operational facility. My primary challenge was building a first-of-its-kind facility in a region with limited infrastructure. To solve this, my role covered three core areas:

  • System Design: I personally designed the entire operational plan, from clinical workflows to international procurement.
  • Partner Management: I managed the key partnerships between international funders (MSD for Mothers), local implementers (Partners In Health), and the Ministry of Health (MoHS).
  • Team Building: I built the local project team, hiring and training them to run the centre independently for long-term success.
