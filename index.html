About Me

I believe that the biggest challenges, whether in healthcare or technology, are solved by building better systems. My expertise lies in taking a complex operational problem, designing a practical, human-centred solution, and leading the cross-functional teams needed to implement it at scale.

Over the past five years, I have built and optimised the operational backbone for LifeBank, a high-growth health technology company. This involved everything from designing international programme rollouts to overhauling logistics networks to drive efficiency. I thrive in fast-paced environments where I can create order from chaos, work with stakeholders at all levels, and deliver measurable results.

Portfolio: Key Projects

Last-Mile Medical Delivery Initiative

Partners: Gates Foundation, BOAT Foundation

The Challenge

To solve the critical issue of medicine stockouts at remote Primary Healthcare Centres across three Nigerian states, which directly impacted community health and trust in the healthcare system.

My Role & Approach

As the project lead, I designed and implemented the end-to-end logistics and operational system. My approach involved building a hybrid model that integrated third-party logistics partners with our core team and deploying new technology to improve digital adoption among healthcare workers. This required intensive on-the-ground training and continuous process refinement to ensure success.

Key Outcomes

  • 85% increase in supply chain visibility.
  • Delivery of over 45,000 essential medicines.
  • Significant reduction in stockouts at partner facilities.

Sierra Leone's First Automated Blood Screening Centre

Partners: Partners In Health (PIH), MSD for Mothers

The Challenge

To improve maternal health outcomes in Sierra Leone by providing reliable, local access to safe blood in a region with limited advanced health infrastructure.

My Role & Approach

I spearheaded this project from the ground up. I managed the entire lifecycle, from the initial needs assessment and co-designing the workflow with local officials, to managing international partner relationships, procuring specialist equipment, and overseeing the training of the local technical team.

Key Outcomes

  • Established the first facility of its kind in the nation.
  • Provides safe blood access for over 5,000 women annually.
  • Created a new, sustainable standard for healthcare in the country.
